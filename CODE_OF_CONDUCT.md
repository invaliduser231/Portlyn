# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in the Portlyn project should remain respectful, direct, and technically constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- focus on facts, code, and outcomes
- challenge ideas without attacking people
- assume good intent until shown otherwise
- keep discussions relevant to the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- abusive or discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- publication of private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate contributions and discussions that violate this
code of conduct.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other official project spaces.
